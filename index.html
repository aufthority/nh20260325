How You're Graded

Your Hero Score is the average of all four category percentages. Each category uses weighted habits — complete foundational ones (⭐⭐⭐) to unlock the full gauge.

S
100% — Perfect Day All four neurochemicals fully activated. Every habit completed across all categories.
A
80–99% — Excellent Strong across the board. All foundational habits done, most secondaries completed.
B
60–79% — Good Foundational habits complete in most categories. Building solid momentum.
C
40–59% — Okay Some habits done but foundational gaps remain. Focus on ⭐⭐⭐ habits first.
D
20–39% — Low Minimal habits completed. Pick one category and start there.
—
0–19% — Not Started A new day waiting to be shaped. Any habit is better than none.

Scoring Rules

Raw category score = sum of all completed habit weights ÷ total possible weights × 100%.

Foundational Gate: If any ⭐⭐⭐ habit is incomplete, the category gauge is capped at 50% — but all habits remain clickable and count when the gate opens.

Flex Mode: Long-press or tap the Flex button on eligible habits to reduce the weight requirement (e.g., ⭐⭐⭐ → ⭐⭐ or ⭐⭐⭐ → ⭐). Useful on harder days.

Hero Score = average of all four category effective percentages.
